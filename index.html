Welcome to my portfolio

Jimi Niemi, a third-year game programmering student

My name is Jimi Niemi, and I am currently studying Game Programming at the South-Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences (XAMK). This is my third year of study, and I am graduating in the spring of 2027.

So far, Unity Engine has been my main development platform, and my goal has been to become familiar with all of its tools. I have worked on procedural generation, shaders, DOTS, UI, object pooling, and many other game systems. Currently, I am more focused on learning the ins and outs of Unreal Engine 5, as I find it better suited for developing PC games.

Developing PC games is the field I am most interested in pursuing in the future. This portfolio provides insight into the projects I have worked on, the level of expertise I have attained, and the types of game ideas I want to develop further.
