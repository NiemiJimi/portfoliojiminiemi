Welcome to my portfolio

Jimi Niemi, a third-year game programmer at XAMK

My name is Jimi Niemi, and I am currently studying Game Programming at the South-Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences (XAMK). This is my 3rd year and I am graduating in the spring of 2027. Unity Engine has been my main platform to work on and my goal is to get to know all of its tools. I have worked on procedural generation, shaders, DOTS, UI, object pooling and many other systems. Currently I am more keen on learning the ins and outs of Unreal Engine 5 because I find it better for developing PC games. Developing PC games is the field I am most working with in the future. This portfolio provides an insight into the projects I have worked on, and the level of expertise I have attained and what kind of game ideas I came up with, that I want to develope further.
