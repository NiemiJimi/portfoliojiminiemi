Golden Drift is a first-person shooter that combines high-speed vehicle combat with competitive multiplayer gameplay. The objective is to control the golden car and score points for your team. Teams consists of six players they are given various vehicles, weapons and gadgets to either defend or attack the golden car depending which team controls it. Working on this project alone and currently only basic elements implemented.
